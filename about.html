About Our Club

Our club has been the heart of the community, fostering talent and passion for hurling for over 50 years
Drumree GAA is growing in numbers every year with great promise for future adult teams. Our Adult team are top tier competitors in the Intermediate championship.

Club History

Drumree have a long association with the GAA dating back to Merrywell/St Martins in 1887, Drumree Hurling and Camogie Club has a rich history of competitive success and community engagement. Over the years, our club has nurtured many skilled players, winning regional championships and representing our community with pride. Our commitment to excellence has made us a pillar of local sportsmanship and camaraderie.
